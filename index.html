Core Strengths

Built for Speed and Security

No bloated plugins, no cloud syncing, no subscription fees. Just pure clipboard utility at your command.

Instant Hotkey Access

Press your customizable global hotkey (default: ⌘⌥C) from anywhere, and the history panel flies out right at your cursor position instantly.

Smart Auto-Paste

Stop manually pressing paste. Simply select your snippet from the list, click or hit Enter, and the app automatically focus-recovers and pastes it.

Intuitive Smart Previews

Intelligently distinguishes and formats items: previews Hex color codes with color swatches, displays shortened URL domain badges, renders image clippings, and formats raw code snippets.

100% Offline & Private

Your data stays yours. All clipboard items are stored locally inside the system's sandbox via UserDefaults. There is no cloud storage, analytics tracking, or internet connection required.

Keyboard Navigation Native

Designed with heavy keyboard users in mind. Navigate through logs using ↑ and ↓ arrows, press Enter to copy/paste, and dismiss instantly using Esc.

Ultra Lightweight

Written in native Swift using SwiftUI and Carbon APIs. Consumes negligible CPU cycles and memory. Tailor storage limits (up to 50 items) to prevent disk inflation.

Workflow

A Seamless Three-Step Loop

Integrate clipboard automation without interrupting your coding flow.

1

Copy Freely

Use your standard Command+C shortcuts across websites, documents, or image editors. CopyPaste automatically records it in the background.

2

Trigger Panel

Press ⌘⌥C. The panel slides out directly next to your cursor, meaning you never have to scan across your wide screen monitor.

3

Press Enter

Use arrow keys to highlight any snippet, and hit Enter. The application closes, refocuses your text editor, and pastes the code instantly.
